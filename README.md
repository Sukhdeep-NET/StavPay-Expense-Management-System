# StavPay - Enterprise Expense Management Platform

## Overview

StavPay is an enterprise-grade SaaS platform built for alternative asset managers, private equity firms, hedge funds, family offices, and real estate organizations. The platform automates the complete business spend lifecycle including invoice processing, expense allocation, approvals, accruals, budgeting, and payment management.

The solution enables organizations to streamline financial operations, improve compliance, reduce manual effort, and gain complete audit visibility across all business spending activities.
